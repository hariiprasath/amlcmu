About Us

The Applied Machine Learning Club at Carnegie Mellon University focuses on providing opportunities for students to apply machine learning techniques to real-world problems.

We establish collaborations with local companies and faculty to identify the challenges that they face. We bring together people from both machine learning and other academic fields to provide an interdisciplinary approach to solving these problems.

Our Mission

Educate.

By hosting events we want to fill the gap between machine learning in an academic context and real-world challenges that people face in industrial & professional settings.

Develop.

Our goal is to equip students with the skills to develop socially cognizant, problem-focused technological solutions to tackle the most challenging problems.

Empower.

By actively engaging with numerous nonprofit organizations in the development of sustainable artificial intelligence solutions we want to empower communities around us.
